IDS log analysis involves reviewing and interpreting logs generated by Intrusion Detection Systems to identify potential security threats. These logs capture events such as:

  • Unusual login attempts
  • Exploits of known software vulnerabilities
  • Denial-of-service (DoS) attacks

By analyzing these logs, security teams can detect and respond to threats promptly.
